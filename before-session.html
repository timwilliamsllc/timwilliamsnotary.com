Checklist: What You Need to Prepare

  • Valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID (Driver’s License, Passport, State ID)
  • Device with camera + microphone (phone, tablet, or computer)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Your documents ready to upload (PDF preferred)
  • Quiet, well-lit location for identity verification

Before You Join the Session

  • Ensure your device is charged or plugged in
  • Have your ID physically with you
  • Close other apps or browser tabs to avoid interruptions
  • Be prepared to answer identity verification questions
