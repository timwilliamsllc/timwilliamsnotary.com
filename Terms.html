Use of Services

By scheduling or participating in a Remote Online Notary (RON) session with Tim Williams LLC, you agree to comply with all Missouri notarial laws and identity verification requirements.

Client Responsibilities

  • You must provide accurate information during identity verification.
  • You must use a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID.
  • You must ensure your documents are complete and ready for notarization.

Notarial Limitations

A notary public cannot provide legal advice, draft legal documents, or determine the legality of a document. The notary’s role is limited to verifying identity and witnessing signatures.

Session Recordings

All RON sessions are audio-video recorded and securely stored as required by Missouri law. These recordings cannot be deleted before the legally required retention period.

Refusal of Service

Tim Williams LLC may refuse service if identity cannot be verified, if the signer appears unwilling or coerced, or if the document is incomplete or inappropriate for notarization.
